Buenos Aires, Argentina

Buenos Aires, the capital of Argentina, is home to over 3 million people and is one of the largest cities in Latin America. Known for its European-inspired architecture, the city features iconic landmarks like the 67-meter-tall Obelisco and the historic Teatro Colón, one of the world’s top opera houses. Buenos Aires is also the birthplace of tango, and its neighborhoods, such as La Boca and San Telmo, are steeped in the dance’s history. The city boasts a rich culinary scene, famous for its asado (barbecue) and Italian dishes like pizza and pasta. With a mix of modern skyscrapers, tree-lined boulevards, and lush parks, Buenos Aires combines old-world charm with contemporary flair, making it a dynamic cultural capital.
